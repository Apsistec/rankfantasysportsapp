Privacy Policy

Rank Fantasy Sports ("rankfantasysports.com & RF$™") has established a privacy policy ("Policy") to offer a more transparent view into how your information is collected, where it is used, and ultimately how it is protected. RF$ may update this policy at any time. RF$ will provide notice of materially significant changes to this privacy policy by posting notice on the RF$ site.

Protecting Your Privacy

RF$ makes all attempts to protect your private information except as provided in this Policy. RF$ will not share your personal information with any third parties without your consent except as provided in this Policy.

Posts on RF$'s website may provide links to third party websites, where each has it's own privacy practices. RF$ is not responsible for, nor does RF$ have any control over the privacy policies of those third parties. Thus RF$ encourages all users to read the privacy policies of each and every website visited.

Data Collection

RF$ may collect your name, age, date of birth, gender, email address, physical address, profile information, photos, and phone number.

RF$ sometimes collects your email address, for purposes such as sending email newsletters, notifications about your account, notifications from other RF$ users, and authenticating your account. By submitting your email RF$, you consent to emails from RF$.

RF$ sometimes collects your cell phone number for the purpose of sending you text messages about RF$. By submitting your cell phone number to RF$, you consent to text messages from RF$.

RF$ may collect personal information if you provide such information in feedback or comments or if you contact RF$ directly. Only information that is directly sent to RF$ may be confidential. Any information posted on the site is not confidential. Please do not post anything on RF$ that you would like to keep private, as it is a public forum. For instance, please do not post your home address on your profile unless you want your home address to be public.

RF$'s web logs collect standard web log entries for each page served, including your IP address, page URL, and timestamp. Web logs help RF$ diagnose problems with our server, to administer the RF$ site, and to otherwise provide RF$'s products and service to you. In addition, RF$ may collect other information from your interaction with our Website, services, content, and advertising, including computer and connection information, statistics on page views, traffic to and from the Website, ad data, IP address, demographic and navigational data, and standard web log information. This information may be obtained for RF$ from a third party provider. RF$ may use this information for marketing, commercial, or any other purpose as RF$ sees fit.

RF$ may collect your geographical location if your device allows it. This information may be posted on the RF$ if you have allowed locations services to be enabled.

Data RF$ Stores

All information posted to this site, including account and purchase information, is stored in RF$'s database, even after "deletion", and may be archived elsewhere.

RF$'s web logs and other records are stored indefinitely. This may include any information you supply to RF$ and your browsing history. However, RF$ cannot guarantee access to these records by third parties for more than seven business days after the original information was submitted, except for purchase information that is available until the items have been delivered.

Although RF$ makes good faith efforts to store personal information in a secure operating environment that is not available to the public, RF$ cannot guarantee complete security. While we also use security to secure the site, any information posted on your public RF$ profile and any discussion boards are made available to the public.

RF$ may store information in an off-site location, which may or may not be located in the United States and may not be bound by the laws of the United States.

Search Engines & 3rd Parties

Search engines and third party sites not affiliated with RF$ - including Google.com, Bing.com, and Yahoo.com - archive or otherwise make available RF$'s web pages. RF$ has no control over third party archiving and search engine sites and cannot guarantee the accuracy of any information stored by search engine sites.

Release of Information

RF$ may disclose information about its users for publicity purposes pursuant to RF$'s Terms of Use. RF$ may disclose information about its users if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process.

RF$ may disclose information about its users to law enforcement officers or others, in the good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to: enforce our Terms of Use; respond to claims that any posting or other content violates the rights of third- parties; or protect the rights, property, or personal safety of RF$, its users, or the general public.

RF$ may disclose your information to third parties for use by RF$. This may include disclosure to email marketing providers to provide targeted marketing for our users or other information to third party marketers. RF$ may disclose your information to other business entities, should RF$ ever plan to merge with or be acquired by that business entity.

International Users

RF$ welcomes international users to browse the site. International Website Users consent to all the terms of service and this privacy agreement. International users waive any and all remedies that they may have based on the laws of their country.
