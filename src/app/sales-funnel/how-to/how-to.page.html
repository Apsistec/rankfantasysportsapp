Instructions for Purchase & Usage of the RF$™ App

  1. Click on the ☰ "hamburger menu" in the top left corner of this app at www.rankfantasysports.com.

  2. Click the 7 day "Free Trial” menu item button.

  3. Pick which Pro package you want:
    1. Bronze package $9.99 per month
    2. Silver package $50 semi-annually (every 6 months)
    3. Gold package $90 annually

  4. Create an account with your name, email address and password, or use your Social Media credentials.

  5. Now enter in your payment details and submit. Payment is complete when you land on the "Welcome Page". Congratualations, welcome aboard!

  6. To View the Tables: Now, click the ☰ "hamburger menu" in the top left corner & click the "Happening Now" menu item button to see "Today’s DraftKings Value Rankings".

  7. Click the Tennis tab to see all Men’s & Women’s tennis DK cheat sheets & stat models.

  8. Click the NFL tab for access to the football DK cheat sheets & stat models.

  9. Click the CFB tab to get access to our college football Power Rankings & Score Prediction cheat sheets.

  10. Click the PGA tab for access to all golf DK cheat sheets & stat models.

  11. Click the NBA tab for access to all basketball DK cheat sheets & stat models.
