How To Use the RFS App

Instructions for Purchasing a Subscription & Accessing the Data

  1. Click the "Signup with Facebook" Button or the "Signup Now" button on the Home page, and create your account login details.

  2. Choose a Pro Package (Gold, Silver, or Bronze)

  3. Enter your credit or debit card details as payment, and then press the "Start Subscription" button.
    Your payment is complete when you land on the "Welcome Page". Congratualations, welcome aboard!

  4. To view all available tables:
    On Desktop: From the home page click the "Stats & Projections" menu button.
    On Mobile: From any page, click the ☰ "hamburger menu" & then click the "All Tables" button next to "Stats & Projections".

  5. Click Happening Now to access Today's DK Value Rankings & Cheatsheets.

  6. Click Tennis to access Men’s & Women’s Tennis DK Cheatsheets and Stat Models.

  7. Click NFL to access Football DK Cheatsheets & Stat Models.

  8. Click CFB to access College Football Power Rankings & Score Prediction Cheatsheets.

  9. Click PGA to access Golf DK Cheatsheets & Stat Models.

  10. Click NBA to access Basketball DK Cheatsheets & Stat Models.
